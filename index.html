Our Story

Effortless Style, Curated Locally

Chic Boutique has been Northfield's go-to spot for on-trend clothing, shoes, and accessories. We hand-select every piece with our customers in mind — pieces that mix easily into a real wardrobe, from everyday essentials to date-night statements.

Stop in and you'll always find something new: fresh arrivals landing weekly, a team that remembers your size and your style, and the kind of personal attention you just don't get shopping online.
